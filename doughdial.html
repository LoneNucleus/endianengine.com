Decarboxylation Guide

Focused adult workflow

This screen is intentionally separate from the recipe calculator. Decarboxylation is a preparation process that may take about 70–75 minutes including oven stabilization and stirring steps.

Temperature Target

Actual oven temperature
230–240°F / 110–116°C
Use an oven thermometer. Do not rely only on the oven dial.
  • Preheat with an oven thermometer inside for about 30 minutes.
  • If the actual oven is above 240°F / 116°C, reduce heat and re-check.
  • At 250°F+ / 121°C+, stop and let the oven cool before continuing.

Reference image only. Use an oven thermometer and the written target range above.
