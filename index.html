3D Dynamic Lattice Nuclide Viewer v0.5.5.0

Launch Demo

An interactive 3D scatter plot of 3,342 known nuclides spanning Z=0 through 118 (Hydrogen through Oganesson). Dynamic axis selection, geometry presets, decay-mode coloring, decay arrows with hover detail, and expanded filtering. Neutron-rich isotopes glow in blues. Proton-rich burn in reds and oranges. Alpha emitters shine gold. Stable nuclides stand as solid silver spheres.

Platform: Web (Plotly/Dash)

// About Endian Engine

Endian Engine is a software studio based in Kalamazoo, Michigan. We build tools that make complex data accessible — from nuclear physics to cryptocurrency to census demographics.

Founded in 2026 by Todd Clifton, we believe that the best software transforms structured data into visual insight that anyone can explore.

Our tools are built with obsessive attention to accuracy and visual clarity. Whether it's 3,342 nuclides plotted in 3D or every county in America mapped by demographic data, we don't cut corners on the data.

And yes, the CEO talks to AI all day. The software writes itself. Sort of.
