Audio Clock

A small desktop utility that speaks the time so you can stay aware of the hour without staring at the clock. Useful while coding, gaming, cooking, resting, or wandering through a task for three hours and pretending that was the plan.

Time, spoken gently enough to be useful and annoying enough to work.

Focus timer? Not exactly. Alarm clock? Not really. A spoken time-awareness tool? Yes.
The idea

Keep time present without breaking focus.

Most clocks ask you to look away from what you are doing. Audio Clock does the small boring job of saying the time out loud at intervals, using the Windows text-to-speech voices already installed on your computer. Start with the free demo, then choose the edition that fits your voice and language setup.
